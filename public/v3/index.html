The Firm

Who is AuditSpherre?

AuditSpherre is a specialist audit and compliance firm focused exclusively on India's regulated financial ecosystem. We work with stockbrokers, depository participants, and financial technology companies to ensure they meet their mandatory audit obligations.

Our auditors bring deep expertise in SEBI regulations, exchange compliance frameworks, and cybersecurity standards including ISO 27001, CERT-In guidelines, and the SEBI Cyber Security & Cyber Resilience Framework.

  • SEBI empanelled — NSE, BSE, MCX, NCDEX experience
  • CISA, CEH, and ISO 27001 Lead Auditor certifications
  • 100% on-time delivery across 200+ engagements
  • Confidential — all clients under NDA from day one
  • Post-audit remediation support included as standard
