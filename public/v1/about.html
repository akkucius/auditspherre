The Firm

Who is Auditspherre?

Auditspherre is a specialist audit and compliance firm focused exclusively on India's regulated financial ecosystem. We work with stockbrokers, depository participants, and financial technology companies to ensure they meet their mandatory audit obligations — without disruption to daily operations.

Our auditors bring deep expertise in SEBI regulations, exchange compliance frameworks, and cybersecurity standards including ISO 27001, CERT-In guidelines, and the SEBI Cyber Security & Cyber Resilience Framework. Every engagement is staffed by professionals who understand the securities market — not generalists borrowing from IT services playbooks.

We believe auditing should be more than a checkbox exercise. Every engagement we undertake is designed to identify real risks, provide practical recommendations, and leave your organisation stronger and more inspection-ready than when we arrived.

Where We Specialise

Three pillars of our practice

Regulatory Knowledge

Deep familiarity with SEBI circulars, exchange bye-laws, IRDAI and RBI frameworks. We track regulatory changes so your audit is always aligned to the current mandate.

Cybersecurity Depth

Certified ethical hackers and information security auditors holding CISA, CEH, and OSCP credentials. Our VAPT methodology is tailored to financial sector attack surfaces.

Financial Domain

Auditors who understand the securities market operationally — order management, settlement cycles, margin mechanics — not just generic IT compliance frameworks.
